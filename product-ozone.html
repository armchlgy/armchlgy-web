THE STORY BEHIND

Riding a motorcycle under the scorching 34°C Jakarta sun destroys 90% of traditional perfumes, turning them into a sour, nauseating mess. The Ozone Shield was engineered to cheat thermodynamics. It uses synthetic hydrophobic molecules that strictly reject human sweat. Instead of oxidizing and breaking down, the Ambroxan uses your extreme body heat as an engine—projecting an intensely cold, sterile aura that cuts straight through the dense smog and humidity.

THE OLFACTIVE ARCHITECTURE

  • Base (The Anchor): Iso E Super, Ambroxan. The pure synthetic shield. Completely inert to sweat, projecting a relentlessly clean geometry.

THE ARCHITECTURE OF RECEIPT

A psychological weapon must be housed accordingly. We offer three tiers of structural presentation based on your intent.

TIER I

THE CORE

An aggressively minimal entry point. Crafted from entirely matte black folding carton and delivered in a simple twisted-handle paper carrier. It is an accessible, stripped-down housing that refuses to compromise on the stark, silent identity of A.G.

TIER II

THE SIGNATURE

The standard formulation. A heavy, robust 2-piece setup cardboard box that snaps shut with absolute finality. Delivered in a thick black cotton canvas bag featuring flat premium ribbon handles. Designed for the executive who values structure over flash.

TIER III

THE SYNDICATE

We rejected the flimsy, high-gloss cardboard of mass luxury. The Syndicate tier is a true presentation vault. A custom rigid setup box draped in pitch-black, deeply textured cotton paper, sealed with black wax. Carried out into the world via a monolithic, heavy woven cotton bag with thick braided rope handles.
